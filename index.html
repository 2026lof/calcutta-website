A modern take on Indian comfort food

Calcutta Wrap & Roll brings the warmth of Indian street food into a cleaner, more elevated restaurant experience with rich spices, fast service, and a strong local identity.

Every dish is designed to feel generous, flavorful, and satisfying — from wraps and rice bowls to tandoori favorites and signature curries.

The goal is simple: serve food that feels authentic, memorable, and easy to order again.
